Frequently Asked Questions

Taurus DEX prioritizes security above all else. Our platform undergoes regular security audits by leading blockchain security firms, implements multi-layer security protocols, and operates on a non-custodial model where users maintain control of their private keys and assets at all times.

Taurus charges a competitive 0.3% fee on all trades, with 0.25% going to liquidity providers and 0.05% to the platform treasury for ongoing development and maintenance. There are no hidden fees or charges.

To start trading, simply connect your Web3 wallet (like MetaMask or Trust Wallet) to our platform, ensure you have the appropriate cryptocurrency for trading, and navigate to our Swap page to begin exchanging tokens.
