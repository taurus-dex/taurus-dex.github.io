The Taurus Journey

Born from the vision of a decentralized future, Taurus was founded to empower individuals with true financial freedom. Inspired by the strength and stability of the Taurus constellation, our platform bridges the cosmos and finance, offering a secure, transparent, and elegant DeFi experience.

We believe in a world where everyone can trade, invest, and grow without boundaries. Our team of blockchain experts, financial analysts, and UX designers have come together to create a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with an intuitive user experience.

Development Roadmap

2022: Concept & Research

The Taurus concept was born from a team of blockchain enthusiasts who envisioned a more accessible and elegant DeFi platform. Initial research and market analysis shaped our core vision.

2023: Development & Testing

Our development team built the Taurus platform from the ground up, focusing on security, speed, and user experience. Extensive testing and auditing ensured a robust foundation.

2024: Launch & Expansion

Taurus officially launched to the public, bringing our vision of elegant, accessible DeFi to life. Our roadmap includes expanding token offerings, adding advanced trading features, and growing our global community.

Q3 2025: MVP Launch

Launch MVP version featuring intent input → aggregated routing execution → AI trend strategy recommendations. Integrate with mainstream DEX aggregators, supporting multi-chain swaps across EVM, Solana, and zkSync networks.

Q4 2025: CopyTrade & Governance

Launch CopyTrade module supporting address following, strategy copying, and portfolio tracking. Introduce incentive system and DAO governance ranking with reward mechanisms.

Q1 2026: Strategy Marketplace

Open strategy marketplace for community-submitted AI strategies and custom parameter templates. Launch Solver execution network with node bidding slot model.

Q2-Q3 2026: Advanced Features

Beta launch of on-chain US stock trading module with tokenized stock trading and compliant KYC integration. Deploy proprietary AI models to reduce OpenAI API dependency with local inference support.

Q2-Q3 2026: Mobile Ecosystem

Official release of Taurus mobile device with pre-installed Wallet, Strategy, and CopyTrade modules. Integrate mobile task system with DAO modules, creating a "hardware × strategy × governance" ecosystem loop.

Q4 2026: Ecosystem Expansion

Full deployment of advanced CopyTrade features and comprehensive DAO governance system. Complete the transformation into a full-stack DeFi ecosystem with hardware integration and community-driven growth.
